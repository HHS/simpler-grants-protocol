---

The Community Stewardship Group brings together organizations from across the grants ecosystem who are helping shape the future of CommonGrants. These are partners collaborating with us on the protocol, its tooling, and the path toward community governance.

We are grateful to everyone building this in the open with us, and the group continues to grow. Interested in getting involved? You can [vote on priorities](https://commongrants.fider.io) on our co-planning board or [get in touch](https://forms.gle/XUJuEnNtaZkdc1MQ6).
