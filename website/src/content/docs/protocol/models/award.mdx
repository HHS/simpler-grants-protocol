## AwardBase

A grant award, tracking the funder, recipient, and financial details of a grant that resulted from an opportunity. It references the source [opportunity](/protocol/models/opportunity#oppref) and [application](/protocol/models/application#appref), and its funders and recipient organizations via [OrgRef](/protocol/models/organization#orgref).
